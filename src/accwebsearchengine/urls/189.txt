On this page you’ll find information on a variety of topics from the Health and Physical Education Curriculum. Each topic includes information on the impacts on the health of students, why it is an issue for our local students, effective strategies for addressing healthy habits, and links to credible organizations for more information. You can also access our Curriculum Supports Page for lesson plans, toolkits, and ideas for teaching the curriculum in a fun and engaging way.
