Main Page Content What are example awareness raising activities to promote physical activity? Distribute credible information on your workplace wellness bulletin boards, by e-mail or on your intranet such as: 24-Hour Movement & Activity Guidelines Canadian Sedentary Behavior Guidelines City of Windsor Parks, Trails, and Recreation Maps County Wide Active Transportation Route Maps Factsheet on stretching at the workstation from the Canadian Centre for Occupational Health and Safety Handbook on exercise at your workstation from the Occupational Health Clinics for Ontario Workers Inc. Municipal Activity Guides or local physical activity or recreation programs Motivational posters encouraging employees to make active choices (e.g., taking the stairs instead of the elevator) What are example education and skill-building activities promote physical activity? Host a Lunch & Learn or an education session led by: Bike Windsor Essex Coordinate active events for employees such as: Walking breaks or an active break workplace challenge The UpnGo.ca Program to help deskbound employees be more active Employee sports teams or activity groups An employee team for the Big Bike for Heart and Stroke or Ride Don’t Hide (June 23) Windsor-Essex County Health Unit’s Online Physical Activity Challenge in April to June A Bike to Work event for Bike Month in June Employee attendance at the County of Essex’s County Wide Active Transportation System Celebrations Event in June Employee noon hour walks for In-Motion Week during the first week of October Host an archived Healthy at Work webinar from the Windsor-Essex County Health Unit: Getting Started on Your Personal Exercise Journey Cycling For Fun Health & Profit What are example environmental supports promote physical activity? Introduce walking meetings. Invest in showers and change rooms to support physical activity and active transportation. Provide corporate or reduced rate memberships to onsite, private, or local fitness or recreation facilities. Have physical activity equipment available to sign out for use during breaks. Have designated areas available that are safe for physical activity (e.g., basketball courts or greenspace) Host on-site fitness or yoga classes. Have safe bicycle storage, such as bicycle racks or bicycle lockers. What are example supportive policies promote physical activity?
