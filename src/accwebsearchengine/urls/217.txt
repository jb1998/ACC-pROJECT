What Are Opioids? What Are Opioids? What are Opioids? Opioids are a group of drugs that are primarily used to relieve pain. Opioids can be prescribed or used illegally. Common opioids include: Codeine Morphine Oxycodone (e.g. Percocet) Hydromorphone (e.g. Vicodin) Fentanyl Methadone Heroin Fact Sheets Opioids: What are they?
