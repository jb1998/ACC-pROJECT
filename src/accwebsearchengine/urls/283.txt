Receipt Printing for Those Without Health Card Numbers The following can be used to schedule an appointment to have your receipt printed if you do not have a healthcard or did not provide your healthcard number. If you require assistance please call 226-773-2200. You will need to bring two (2) of the following pieces of identification to your appointment: Government issued photo identification (e.g., drivers license, health card, passport) Birth certificate Citizenship card A piece of mail with your name and address A letter from your employer verifying your name Windsor-Essex County Health Unit (Windsor) View/Cancel Appointment
