YoMingo offers you excellent resources for everything you want to know about pregnancy, birth and beyond. Our information is evidence-based, and the platform is easy to navigate. We hope that you feel empowered and reassured, finding all of the tools that you need to learn and prepare for the exciting journey to parenthood! Self-register for our e-learning prenatal program by clicking this link: www.wechu.org/yomingo or register by contacting us at 519-258-2146 ext. 1350. The best time to contact us is 4 months before your baby is due. In-Class Prenatal Breastfeeding Course Due to COVID-19, Prenatal Breastfeeding Classes are on hold until further notice. Our prenatal breastfeeding course is a free, 2-hour class, provided in partnership by Registered Nurses from the Windsor-Essex County Health Unit and Windsor Regional Hospital Family Birthing Centre. It is designed to give women and their partners the information they need to breastfeed successfully. It is offered twice a month at Windsor Regional Hospital- Metropolitan Campus. Our free, 2-hour prenatal breastfeeding class is also offered at the Windsor-Essex County Health Unit - Leamington Site every other month (February, April, June, August and October). For any infant feeding questions, or to register for this course, call our Healthy Families Hotline at 519-258-2146 ext. 1350. Client Feedback Survey Give us feedback!
