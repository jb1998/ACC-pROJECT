Contact Us Contact Us We're open at all locations from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday, except for statutory holidays. Free parking is available at all of our locations. You can contact us by phone, fax, or through our website, or through social media on Facebook [1] and Twitter [2]. Our phone number is: 519-258-2146 (local) or 1-800-265-5822 (toll free). The fax numbers provided below are for non-confidential information. To find out how to send us a confidential fax, please call us at 519-258-2146 for assistance. Locations Impacted by COVID-19 Our Windsor office is open Monday to Friday from 8:30 a.m. until 4:30 p.m. Please note that our Leamington Office will accept private residential well water samples for coliform and E.coli testing, but all other services are cancelled. The Essex office is closed.
